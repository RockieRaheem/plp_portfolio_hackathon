About Me

Hello! I'm Kamwanga Raheem, a passionate 23-year-old Software Engineering student at Uganda Technology and Management University (UTAMU). My journey in technology is driven by an unwavering commitment to creating innovative solutions that bridge the gap between cutting-edge development and environmental sustainability.

As a dedicated Full Stack Developer, I specialize in building robust web applications using modern frameworks like React, Next.js, and Node.js. My expertise spans across the entire development lifecycle, from crafting intuitive user interfaces to architecting scalable backend systems. I'm particularly fascinated by Green Software practices, constantly exploring ways to minimize digital carbon footprints while maximizing performance.

Beyond coding, I believe in maintaining a balanced lifestyle. I find strength and discipline through boxing and regular gym sessions, which enhance my problem-solving mindset. Soccer fuels my competitive spirit and teamwork skills, while music serves as my creative catalyst during development sessions. This blend of technical expertise and personal passions drives my mission to make a meaningful impact on the world through technology.
